About Me

My name is Zheng Yu. I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Northwestern University , advised by Prof. Xinyu Xing . My research interest includes software security, kernel security, IoT security and smart contracts .


Before joining Northwestern, I obtained a bachelor's degree from the ACM Honor Class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, majoring in Computer Science. During my undergrad, I was very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xinyu Xing at Northwestern, Prof. Yinqian Zhang at SUSTech, and Prof. Chao Li at SJTU.


Besides, I am very interested and good at algorithm design, I was an Oier when I was study in Yali. I am also a CTF player, I used to play CTF with team StrawHat.
