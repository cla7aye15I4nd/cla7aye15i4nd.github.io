About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Northwestern University, advised by Prof. Xinyu Xing. My research focuses on System Security and AI Security, with the goal of enhancing program security with minimal human intervention.

I received my bachelor's degree from the ACM Honor Class at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I was advised by Prof. Chao Li. I also had the privilege of working with Prof. Meng Xu at the University of Waterloo.

Beyond research, I have a passion for algorithm design, competitive programming (former OIer from Yali High School), and CTF competitions (Nu1L, StrawHat). I also enjoy traveling, watercolor painting, and sketching.
